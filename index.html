About our
tailoring house

We, Fleece Overflow are reputed and well known experts in the tailoring field. Our provided services are garment manufacturing, embroidery services. We also sell sewing accessories.

Our main areas of specialization are School Uniforms, Security Uniforms, Protective Clothing, Hospitality Uniforms, Health Care Clothing (Scrubs, e.t.c), Promotional Garments, Custom Event Garments (Birthday t-shirts, Maroora Garments & Funeral/Manyaradzo t-shirts). Our skilled tailors handle all of our cutting and sewing ensuring precision in all garment production processes including in-house branding.
